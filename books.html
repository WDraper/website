Unfortunatley, this isnt a page listing all the books I have written. Instead it is a list of books I am or have read. I am in the middle of somesort of mid-youth crisis. The cusp/begining of adulthood, wondering if I am choosing the right path. Naturally as a trained computer science student when faced with questions I turned to google. After more self help articles than I would care to admit, I have gained some confidence that it really doesnt matter how I start these first few years. But while digging through click-bait articles of "How not to screw up your 20's" I started to find some common advice. READ. Read a lot. Even HBR says you should read. I would never have called myself a reader. Never really read other than what was required for classes, sometimes not even that. So after my first senior year I set out to try and make a new habbit.
